# Disclaimer

This MCP server provides environmental compliance data for informational purposes only.

## Not Legal Advice

The data served by this tool does not constitute legal, regulatory, or professional environmental advice. Environmental regulations are complex and site-specific. NVZ designations change on a 4-year review cycle. Always:

- **Verify your NVZ status** on the DEFRA Magic Map before relying on NVZ rules
- **Contact the Environment Agency** for site-specific requirements
- **Consult a qualified environmental advisor** before making compliance decisions
- **Check current legislation** as regulations are amended periodically

## Currency

Environmental regulations change. This tool shows the data available at the time of its last ingestion run. Check `check_data_freshness` for the age of the data. NVZ designations are reviewed every 4 years.
